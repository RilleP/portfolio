Solo projects

Castle Strike / Bailey bash (C#)

  • Real time multiplayer using deterministic lockstep
  • A* pathfinding
  • Many different units with many different abilities
  • Height based fog of war, where it is possible to see down from walls, but not up. And no seeing though buildings.

Lord of the birds (C++)

  • Smart bird behaviour when building nests and fishing
  • Multiplayer with thousands of birds in real time
  • Computer controlled AI players
  • Procedurally generated map

Vrodl - the marionette (C#)

  • Millions of different randomized items
  • Procedurally generated worlds
  • Story with quests
  • Five unique boss fights
